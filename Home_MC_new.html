Our Story

Founded in 2014, Money Creek Whiskey is our small, family owned distillery in southern Minnesota. Money Creek rolls through our ranch giving rise to the name. The small spring fed creek provides the neccessary, sustainable water for our whiskey. The ranch's bluffs are the perfect place to grow corn and barley providing an ideal place to harvest the ingredients from our own land.
